# Expectations — 12: One huge rambling brain-dump

~450 words of unstructured stream-of-consciousness containing a complete,
unambiguous week: clear progress, one explicitly-signaled HIGH blocker,
one scoped decision with a deadline. Tests compression — the draft must
land under 300 words without losing the key items, per the rubric's
no-paragraphs and bullet rules.

## Expected clarifying question topics

None — a CLARIFY response fails `correct_mode`. The dump is long, not
ambiguous.

## Must include

- Wave 5 outreach complete (all 6 owners confirmed); first 2 onboarding
  sessions done, remaining 4 booked
- Site 41 cleanup finished July 8; closure report accepted
- July metrics: ECD at 84% migrated (up from 78% in February)
- Protocol team / SharePoint blocker tagged **[HIGH]**: read-only date
  September 1, exception unresolved with ECD leadership, August 1
  resolution deadline, protocol team loses edit access to regulatory
  submission documents, blocks Wave 5 scheduling — KM Team Lead
  re-escalation needed this week
- Decision: fold Site 52 back into Wave 5 (~4k files, dependency
  resolved) or keep deferred — KM Team Lead call by July 17

## Must exclude

- Paragraph-style prose (rubric: bullets only)
- Office hours (input marks it "nothing to report")
- Rambling artifacts ("brain dump", "NOW THE BAD PART", "that saga")

## Judge notes

- `word_count` is the sharpest gate here — the input is ~450 words and
  the draft must be under 300 (target 200).
- The metrics were already forwarded to ECD leadership ("no action
  needed"); they belong in Progress, not Decisions.
